Tuesday Snapback Brief

7 July 2026 · 48 names · benchmark QQQ · data 07:26 UTC

Markets are trading again — and Monday delivered a broad snapback. The first real US session since Thursday saw the chip complex V-bounce after its brutal early-July rout (the semis ETF SMH had shed ~5% and roughly $1.3 trillion in a two-day slide), and the rally widened out: Tesla, Bloom Energy and Eaton all flipped up into Leading, AMD and Astera Labs ripped 6%+, and the model logged six status changes — five of them higher. The froth is creeping back (heat is jumping across the leaders) but there's still not a single Overheated name on the board. The one that didn't join the party is the tell: Alphabet flipped the wrong way, into Lagging, as the market keeps punishing its capex bill.
