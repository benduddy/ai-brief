Levels

50-day ~$733; sitting on its 52-week high ~$1,134. The first real shelf of support is far below — a gap that only matters if the print disappoints.

Setup

Entry grade C — best-in-class fundamentals, parabolic entry: wait, don't chase. A 0.10 PEG doesn't cushion a top-of-range buy two days before a coin-flip report. The honest plan is to let Wednesday clear first, then judge the reaction — a flush toward the rising 50-day would be the cleaner re-entry.
