Signal Brief — the optical layer runs hot, the mega-caps cool

23 June 2026 · 48 names · benchmark QQQ · data 08:06 UTC

A genuine rotation day on the board: the connective tissue of the AI build — optical fibre, transceivers, AI servers — ran hot enough to flip three names into Overheated, while the two biggest mega-caps, Nvidia and Alphabet, quietly lost their Leading tag.

  • Nine status changes — the board woke up. After a flat Friday, the universe flipped hard: Corning, Dell and Coherent all crossed into Overheated, while Nvidia dropped from Leading to Neutral and Alphabet from Leading to Basing on a ~5% fall. The money rotated down the stack — out of the mega-caps and into the picks-and-shovels of the data-centre buildout.
  • The optical / networking corner is where the heat went. Corning (Amazon fibre megadeal), Coherent (Nvidia's $2bn equity stake) and Applied Optoelectronics (800G ramp, fresh Neutral→Leading flip, +5.8%) all moved on real datacentre-optics catalysts. This is the "wiring of the AI factory" trade, and it's the day's clear leadership.
  • Wednesday is still the hinge: Micron reports 24 Jun after the close. Street is ~$34.7bn revenue / ~$19.95 EPS with gross margin near 81% — the test of whether the memory supercycle is real or cresting. MU sits parabolic into it (heat 93, ~62% above its 50-day) at the cheapest valuation in the book (PEG ~0.11). A binary print.
  • Entry watchlist — buyable-now is thin. Only QCOM grades A (leading, trend intact, near an entry). The B bucket (add-on-strength) is led by the cheap-for-growth names — DELL, COHR, ASML, TSM, AMD, FLNC, VRT, GEV all carry a forward PEG under ~1.25. The whole memory/parabolic group (MU, SNDK, MRVL, ALAB, ENTG, INTC, BE) stays grade C: strong trend, hot entry, don't chase.
  • Backdrop: hawkish Fed, talent drama at Google. Warsh's first FOMC held at 3.50–3.75% but the dot plot now leans to one hike by year-end (~66% odds); May CPI ran 4.2% (core 2.9%). Alphabet's slide was as much about its top Gemini minds leaving for OpenAI and Anthropic as about the tape.
